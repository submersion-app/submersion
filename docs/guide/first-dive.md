# Your First Dive

Let's log your first dive in Submersion! This quick tutorial will get you familiar with the basics.

## Step 1: Create Your Diver Profile

When you first launch Submersion, you'll be asked to create a diver profile.

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">
  <strong>Screenshot: Welcome/Onboarding Screen</strong><br>
  <em>The welcome screen where you create your first diver profile</em>
</div>

1. Enter your **name**
2. Optionally add your **email** and **phone**
3. Tap **Create Profile**

<div class="tip">
<strong>Tip:</strong> You can add more details to your profile later, including emergency contacts, medical info, and insurance details.
</div>

## Step 2: Open the Dive Log

After creating your profile, you'll land on the **Dives** tab - your main dive log.

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">
  <strong>Screenshot: Empty Dive List</strong><br>
  <em>The dive list with no dives yet, showing the "Log Your First Dive" button</em>
</div>

Tap the **+ Log Dive** button (floating action button) to create a new dive.

## Step 3: Enter Basic Dive Info

The dive entry form has several sections. For your first dive, focus on the basics:

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">
  <strong>Screenshot: Dive Entry Form</strong><br>
  <em>The top portion of the dive entry form</em>
</div>

### Required Fields

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Date & Time** | When the dive started |
| **Max Depth** | Your maximum depth (in meters or feet) |
| **Duration** | Bottom time in minutes |

### Recommended Fields

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Dive Site** | Select or create a site |
| **Water Temperature** | For your records and stats |
| **Visibility** | Excellent, Good, Moderate, Poor |

## Step 4: Select or Create a Dive Site

Tap the **Dive Site** field to select a location:

1. If you have no sites yet, tap **+ Add New Site**
2. Enter the **site name** (e.g., "Blue Corner, Palau")
3. Optionally set the **country** and **region**
4. Tap **Save**

<div class="tip">
<strong>Tip:</strong> You can also tap "Use My Location" to capture GPS coordinates from your current position.
</div>

## Step 5: Add Tank Information

Scroll down to the **Tanks** section:

1. Tap **Add Tank** (or edit the default tank)
2. Set **volume** (e.g., 12L or 80 cu ft)
3. Enter **start pressure** and **end pressure**
4. If diving nitrox, adjust the **O2%** (default is 21% for air)

<div class="screenshot-placeholder">

## Step 6: Add Notes (Optional)

Scroll to the **Notes** section to add any observations:

- Marine life you saw
- Conditions you experienced
- Memorable moments
- Things to remember for next time

## Step 7: Save Your Dive

Tap **Save** in the top right corner.

Your dive is now logged!

## Quick Tips

### Keyboard Shortcuts (Desktop)

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Cmd/Ctrl + N` | New dive |
| `Cmd/Ctrl + S` | Save |
| `Cmd/Ctrl + F` | Search |

### Multi-Diver Support

If you share a device with another diver, you can create multiple profiles:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Divers**
2. Tap **+ Add Diver**
3. Switch between profiles from the same menu

Each diver has their own separate dive log, sites, and gear.
